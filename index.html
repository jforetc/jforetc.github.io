Our Food, and Our Drinks

Our menu is always vegan and gluten free friendly!

Brunch menu features flavours from Mexico and Southern California. Our signature burritos, tacos and in house margaritas.

Our courtyard can be hired for private functions! We can accommodate up to 50 people, are fully licensed and offer a range of catering options.

Boozy brunch? We make our own Micheladas and Margaritas in house. We also stock a range of seasonal artisan Tequila and Mezcal for special occasions!
